Introduction


Hello!

My name is Natalia Cano, and I am currently a senior at Rock Hill High School. I come from a family of 4: my father, mother, and younger brother. I was born in Orlando, Florida, but moved to Texas when I was little. Although I do not plan on majoring or minoring in anything photography-related, I enjoy taking photos. Below are a few of photos taken by me on vacations, college visits, and within my own home. On the left, you can see a photo taken at night of the National Archives in Washington, D.C. This photo is in a college magazine at my local college after it was selected amongst many other talented photographers, and I was very honored to have the opportunity to be included in that. The center photo is an edited photo of my 13-year-old cat, Snowball. I have had her for around 8 years, and I will miss her when I leave for college. Lastly, the image on the right is a picture of a statue at the University of Texas in Austin. I saw the piece of art during a camp hosted by the National Student Leadership Conference, and thought it would make a decent picture, and I was very satisfied with the results.


These are a few things about me, access other areas of this webpage to learn more about who I am, what I do and know, and my plans for the future!
